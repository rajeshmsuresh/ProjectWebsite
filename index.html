CASE STUDIES

INDIA KAWASAKI MOTORS

We helped Kawasaki by designing and developing a training application to serve as the foundation of a new approach to dealer training. The app delivers a variety of interactive training resources to end users, and gives them the ability to customize their own learning process.

The model gallery allows employees to see all of the latest information for a vehicle within two “clicks”. This feature’s elegant design also allows it to be used with the customer if needed.

BAJAJ MOTORCYCLES 

Bajaj was looking for creative ideas to take their Showroom experience to a new level; our team answered in a big way.

A custom Tablet based application was designed from the ground up to offer dynamic content updates, on the fly curriculum adjustments, and real-time event analytics.

DSK Benelli India 

DSK Benelli has been a long-standing Superbike Manufacture company in India. So when it came time for them to re-imagine dealership management, we jumped at the opportunity to expand our relationship.

Our team was responsible for the technology selection, design, development, and maintaining of the DMS (Dealership Management software). We continue to provide monitoring and maintenance services.
